Voltage Divider Sensors

Voltage Dividers


Recall the concept of a voltage divider. You can replace a fixed-value resistor with an analog sensor like a thermistor or Hall sensor. Changing the value of the fixed resistor is a way to tune the output to a particular range.

Potentiometers


A potentiometer is a voltage divider with a movable contact. It can be used to provide a variable voltage, or act as a variable resistance.
