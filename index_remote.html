Note: This page presents the remote-instruction version of PHYSCI 70, which assumes that students will not have lab access. An alternative hybrid version can be found here. The hybrid version reserves some amount of lab time for students to operate machines (CNC, laser cutter and 3D printer). In the remote version, students will learn to prepare digital files for fabrication, which the course staff will then machine and mail to students to incorporate into their projects. The remote version of the course will feature an added emphasis on wireless networking (Internet of Things) and machine building.
