## Final Presentations PHYSCI 70 Final Presentations will be held in SC102 on <b>Thursday May 12th from 3-6pm</b>. Please feel free to invite friends and family. Refreshments will be provided. ### Presentation Format We will share our work in a "project fair" format, where everyone will have one hour to stand by their station and present their work to anyone who comes by. - Please have your class website open on a laptop (or other display). Be ready to demo your final project as best you can. Also, include any weekly assignments that you're proud of, especially kinetic sculptures. - Your grade is mostly based on your documentation and scope of work, but also on your presentation, to a lesser extent.
