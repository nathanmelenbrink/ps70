Final Presentations

PHYSCI 70 Final Presentations will be held at this Zoom Link on Thursday May 13th from 3-4:30pm. Please feel free to share with friends and family. The session will be recorded and hosted on Canvas afterwards.

Presentation Format

Presenters will have 7 minutes each. Presentations should begin with a demo video (up to 1 minute), followed by a brief description of the fabrication process. During the presentation, the audience is invited to post questions to the presenter in the Zoom chat window (if the audience doesn't ask questions, the teaching staff will). Presenters should reserve the final couple minutes of their allotted time to respond to questions. After presentations conclude, the Zoom meeting will remain open, affording the audience and presenters additional time for discussion.
